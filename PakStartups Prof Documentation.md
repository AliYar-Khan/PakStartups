# **1. Project Description**

PakStartups is evolving from a simple subreddit into a structured
startup ecosystem for Pakistan. The goal is to bridge the gap between
early-stage founders, skilled individuals, and real opportunities by
creating a centralized, accessible, and community-driven platform.

The vision is to build a grassroots-level ecosystem that supports:

-   Early-stage founders

-   Students and learners

-   Freelancers and skilled professionals

-   Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs)

-   Grassroots financing and development

Unlike traditional ecosystems that are heavily gatekept or
investor-driven, PakStartups focuses on accessibility, collaboration,
and practical execution.

This is not just a platform --- it is a network-first ecosystem built
on:

-   Community (Reddit, Discord, and social media platforms)

-   Execution (website platform and physical events)

-   Collaboration (founder connections and partnerships)

# **4. What We Are Building**

PakStartups will operate across three interconnected layers:

## **4.1 Community Layer**

-   Subreddit (r/PakStartups)

-   Discord server

-   Social media platforms

-   Weekly online and physical meetups

## **4.2 Platform Layer (PakStartups.org)**

-   Central hub for all activities

-   Structured data and opportunities

-   Founder discovery and interaction

## **4.3 Network Layer**

-   Founder connections

-   B2B partnerships

-   Collaboration pipelines
